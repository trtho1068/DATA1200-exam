PH461 a Strategic Workspace

PH461 serves as the dedicated computer room in building P35. Reserved exclusively for IT students like myself, it stands out as one of my preferred spots. Its generous space and strategically placed power outlets contribute to a comfortable environment. If you appreciate sitting in a corner while staying observant of your surroundings, this is the ideal spot for you.

Elevated Comfort

Discover these inviting sofa spots situated just above the main entrance of the P35 building, offering a picturesque view of Holdbergsplassen. Complete with convenient power outlets and well-designed lighting, this space is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional! Based on my experience, arriving early is a prudent choice, given the popularity of this spot; it tends to get occupied swiftly. Alternatively, you can also consider visiting later in the afternoon or during the weekend for a more relaxed atmosphere.

Personally, I favor sofa seating to alleviate strain on my back. Engaging in IT programming for extended periods can take a toll on your posture, making comfortable and varied seating crucial. Therefore, I have to give the top spot to "elevated comfort" due to its and nice view.
