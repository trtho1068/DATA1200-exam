University Library

The University Library consists of four libraries, a study workshop, a media section and research support.

Opening hours

External visitors have access to the libraries when they are staffed. Staffed hours for the libraries in Pilestredet are as follows:

  • Monday to Thursday: 08:00 AM - 6:30 PM
  • Friday: 08:00 AM - 3:30 PM

For students and staff at OsloMet, the opening hours for the libraries in Pilestredet are as follows:

  • Monday to Sunday: 06:00 AM - 10:00 PM (08:00 AM - 10:00 PM)

Use your card and code for access to the libraries outside staffed hours.
