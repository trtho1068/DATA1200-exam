OsloMet Campus Pilestredet

Welcome! Here we present to you our campus at OsloMet Pilestredet. You will learn about a few of our favorite locations, and on the topical page we will let you peek into one of the many weird interests people may have here.

On the accessibility page you can read about how we used automated accessibility tests while creating this site, to try our best to make it accessible for everyone.

Did you know? In 1994 Høgskolen i Oslo and Høgskolen i Akershus were created, as a result of a now probably ancient reform. In 2011 the two schools were joined to become Høgskolen i Oslo og Akershus, which in 2014 were joined by ten other institutions, greatly strengthening the research part of the school. In february 2017 the school applied to become a university, and in january 2018 the approval was accepted, and OsloMet was born. Source and further reading: lokalhistoriewiki.no
