Top Spots

Finding a suitable spot to sit and work at school can be a challenge at times. It is, however, important to get out of the house and explore different locations.

Here we share with you our favorite spots. Using these varied environments can enhance your learning experience, and this guide makes finding that perfect workspace a more enjoyable task.

Group Rooms

PI230-PI238 and PE238-PE242 are designated group rooms, designed to comfortably accommodate up to six individuals. These rooms offer ease of accessibility and can be effortlessly reserved.

Personally, these are among my favorites, mainly due to their soundproofing and private ambiance. It served as the ideal setting for collaborating with my group during a home exam.

Given its popularity, it's advisable to proactively book the room if you intend to hold a meeting with your group in this environment.

Strategic Workspace

PH461 serves as the dedicated computer room in building P35. Reserved exclusively for IT students like myself, it stands out as one of my preferred spots.

Its generous space and strategically placed power outlets contribute to a comfortable environment. If you appreciate sitting in a corner while staying observant of your surroundings, this is the ideal spot for you.

Elevated Comfort

Discover these inviting sofa spots situated just above the main entrance of the P35 building, offering a picturesque view of Holbergsplassen.

Complete with convenient power outlets and well-designed lighting, this space is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional!

Based on my experience, arriving early is a prudent choice, given the popularity of this spot; it tends to get occupied swiftly. Alternatively, you can also consider visiting later in the afternoon or during the weekend for a more relaxed atmosphere.

I favor sofa seating to alleviate strain on my back. Engaging in IT programming for extended periods can take a toll on your posture, making comfortable and varied seating crucial. I have to give the first place to Elevated Comfort due to its comfort and nice view.
